Privacy Policy for Open Earth Engine

Last updated: 11 July 2023

This Privacy Policy describes how Open Earth Engine ("we", "us", or "our") collects, stores, and uses your information when you install and use our Chrome extension. By using Open Earth Engine, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

The Open Earth Engine extension collects and stores locally the following information:

  • Settings/Preferences: These are user-customized settings within the extension.
  • Planet API Key: If provided by the user, we store the API key locally to enable access to Planet's services as per user preferences.

We do not collect any other personal data nor do we use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

How We Use the Information

The collected information is used exclusively within the extension to enhance your user experience:

  • The settings/preferences are used to customize the functionality of the extension.
  • The Planet API key is used solely for the purpose of accessing Planet's services upon user's request.

Please note, we do not transmit or share this information with any third parties. The data remains within the extension on your local device.

Data Storage

All user data collected by the extension is stored locally on your device. We do not transfer or store your data on any external servers.

Access to Your Information

As a user, you have the ability to view, modify, or delete your data at any time. This can be done through the extension's options page, or removing the extension to delete.

Data Protection

We are committed to protecting your privacy and have taken steps to ensure the security of your information. As your data is stored locally and not transmitted, the risk of data breaches is significantly minimized.

Opting Out

If you wish to opt out of data collection, you can do so by uninstalling the extension. Upon removal, all locally stored data associated with the extension will be automatically erased.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. Please check this page periodically for updates.
